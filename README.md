# Omid Taheri

Postdoctoral Researcher at **MPI-IS** (Michael Black’s group). I work on **Embodied AI**, **Digital Humans**, and **3D interaction**, recently focusing on **GenAI** (diffusion) and grounding **LLMs/VLMs** for tasks like dexterous grasping, avatar motion, and video understanding.

**On the market for Research Scientist roles.**

## Recent work (selected)
- **CLUTCH (ICLR 2026)** — language → hand motion with LLMs/VLMs  
- **NIL (CVPR 2026)** — video diffusion for learning when motion data is missing  
- **InteractVLM (CVPR 2025)** — grounding VLM knowledge into 3D contact & HOI  
- **HaPTIC (3DV 2026)** — consistent 4D hand trajectories from monocular video
