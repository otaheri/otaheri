# Omid Taheri

Postdoctoral Researcher at the **Max Planck Institute for Intelligent Systems (MPI-IS)** in **Michael Black’s group (Perceiving Systems)**. I’m wrapping up my postdoc and am **on the job market for Research Scientist roles**.

My work bridges the **semantic knowledge of foundation models (LLMs/VLMs)** with **structured 3D / physical tasks**—from **dexterous grasping** and **avatar motion** to **hand-object interaction**, **video understanding**, and **garment generation**.

---

## Research interests
- Digital Humans & Avatar Animation  
- Human–Object Interaction (contact, grasping, dexterous manipulation)  
- Diffusion Models for Motion & Video  
- LLM/VLM for Avatar Control & Interaction  
- Text-to-Motion Generation  
- Video Understanding  
- 3D Reconstruction & Representation  
- Embodied AI & Robotics  

---

## Recent work (from my website)
- **NIL (CVPR 2026)** — *No-data Imitation Learning by Leveraging Pre-trained Video Diffusion Models*  
- **HaPTIC (3DV 2026)** — *Predicting 4D Hand Trajectory from Monocular Videos*  
- **CLUTCH (ICLR 2026)** — *Text-conditioned hand motion modeling in the wild*  
- **NGL-Prompter (arXiv 2026)** — *Training-free sewing pattern estimation from a single image*  
- **FUSION (arXiv 2026)** — *Full-body unified motion prior for body and hands via diffusion*  
- **Moving by Looking (arXiv 2025)** — *Vision-driven avatar motion generation (CLOPS)*  
- **Half-Physics (arXiv 2025)** — *Kinematic 3D human model with physical interactions*  
- **InteractVLM (CVPR 2025)** — *3D interaction reasoning from 2D foundational models*  

Full list of publications & project pages: https://otaheri.github.io/publications
